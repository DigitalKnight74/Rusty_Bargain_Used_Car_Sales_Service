# Rusty_Bargain_Used_Car_Sales

Rusty Bargain Used Car Sales service is developing and app to attract new customers. In the app, you can quickly find out the market value of your car.

There is access to historical data:
-  technical specifications
-  trim versions
-  prices

Objective is to build a model to determine the value.

Rusty Bargain is interested in:
-  the quality of the prediction
-  the speed of the prediction
-  the time required for training
